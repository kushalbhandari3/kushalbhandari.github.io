Courseworks

Human Resource Management (BUSM-379)

This project explores how Netflix sparked innovation by transforming its HR practices to foster a culture of freedom and responsibility. By redefining traditional HR paradigms, Netflix revolutionized the entertainment industry and reshaped employee engagement, recruitment, retention, empowerment, and organizational culture. Key concepts covered include peer feedback, collaboration, and strategic benefits.

Business Analytics (BUSM-310)

For my Business Analytics class, I prepared a slide analyzing the impact of Twitter’s rebranding to X and Elon Musk’s leadership on the company’s future revenue streams and user experience. Using predictive, descriptive, and prescriptive analytics, I evaluated potential shifts in monetization strategies, user engagement, and market positioning, leading to key data-driven conclusions.
