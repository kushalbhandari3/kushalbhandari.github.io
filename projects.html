This project explores how Netflix sparked innovation by transforming its HR practices to foster a culture of freedom and responsibility. Key concepts covered include peer feedback, collaboration, and strategic benefits.

Philosophy of Animals

For my Philosophy of Animals class, I explored the obsession of wealthy individuals with animals, analyzing their symbolic role in status, luxury, and dominance. My research covered:

  • Animals as Luxury Goods: Rare and exotic animals serve as status symbols, much like designer brands, enhancing social prestige.
  • Dominance Over Nature: Owning powerful animals reflects control over the natural world and hierarchical status.
  • Conspicuous Consumption: The high cost of these animals increases their perceived value, reinforcing exclusivity.
